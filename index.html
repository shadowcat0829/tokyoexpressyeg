Authentic Japanese Delight at Tokyo Express:
30 Years of Culinary Excellence in Edmonton

Tokyo Express has been Edmonton’s finest Sushi Restaurant for over 30 years! We combine quality, nutritious, and locally sourced fresh ingredients with time-honored and traditional Japanese recipes, that provide our customers with something different from the same old. We offer a wide variety of freshly made popular bento boxes, noodle dishes, and traditional sushi that are sure to please any palate. We pride ourselves on delivering great-tasting and healthy food options at affordable prices. If you haven’t dined with us before, we invite you to visit any of our 11 Edmonton and area locations.
